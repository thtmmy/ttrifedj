What is Safe Heaven Foundation

Safe Heaven Foundation is an international non-profit founded by Celeb Name, over 8 years. We are active in over 100 countries and were the among first international charity for children. We give children in the U.S. and around the world a healthy start in life, the opportunity to learn and protection from harm. When a crisis occurs, we are among the first to respond to an emergency and the last to leave.

How do I volunteer for a specific emergency?

Safe Heaven Foundation has a formal overseas volunteer program during emergencies. We have internal REDI teams (Responding to Emergencies and Disasters Internationally/Domestically) made up of our personnel who train year-round in their various fields of focus and expertise in order to be ready to deploy when disaster strikes.
